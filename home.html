Basics of Programming

Welcome to the world of programming! Here you will learn what programming is, why it’s important, and how different programming languages are used in the modern world.

What is Programming??

Programming is the process of writing instructions that a computer can understand and execute. It is the foundation of all digital technologies, from simple apps on your smartphone to complex control systems in cars and spacecraft. People program using programming languages.

Why is Programming Important?

Today, programming is an integral part of many areas of life. With it, you can create useful applications, automate routine tasks, analyze large amounts of data, and even develop artificial intelligence. Understanding the basics of programming opens the door to many career opportunities and helps you better understand the world around you.

Programming in Everyday Life

Programming affects many aspects of our lives. It underlies all the digital technologies we use daily. From the alarm clock on your phone to the recommendation system on your favorite streaming service — all of this is created using programming.
